# 🛠️ Technical Operations & Industrial Engineering
This folder contains standardized operating procedures for high-performance mechanical systems.

### 🏭 Industrial Process & Thermal Systems
* **Combustion Systems:** Burner tuning, fuel-air ratio optimization, and boiler efficiency.
* **Lubrication Management:** Tribology-based scheduling for heavy machinery (ISO VG grades).
* **Filtration Systems:** Selection protocols for air/liquid filtration (Micron-level accuracy).
* **Heavy Fabrication:** Sizing, selection, and QA/QC for pressure vessels and structural alignment.



### 🏢 Facility O&M & Healthcare
* **HVAC/VRF/Chillers/AHU:** Optimization of KW/TR and descaling protocols.
* **Healthcare (MGPS):** Critical Medical Gas Pipeline maintenance and OT pressure gradients.
* **Heating Solutions:** Selection of Industrial PNG,LPG, Boilers, Heaters and Heat Exchangers.
