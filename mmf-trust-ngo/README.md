🤝 M.M.F. Trust: Social Engineering & Training
The M.M.F. Trust is a Fatehpur-based non-profit dedicated to technical empowerment. We bridge the rural-industrial gap through:

Vocational Training: Hands-on skill development in Mechanical, MEP, and Computer trades for underprivileged youth.

Career Guidance: Strategic consultancy, professional resume building, and industry-specific interview coaching.

Technical Mentorship: Developing a pipeline of certified, job-ready technicians through expert-led training.

CSR Consultancy: Partnering with corporations to design and execute sustainable rural infrastructure and skill-gap programs.

Mission: Transforming local potential into global technical excellence.
