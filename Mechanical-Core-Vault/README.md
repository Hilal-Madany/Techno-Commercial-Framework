## 📐 Application-Based Engineering Logic

### **1. Centralized Lubrication (CLS)**
Focusing on **Nortek** and **Shaanlube** architectures for heavy rotary assets:
* **Dual-Line vs. Progressive Systems:** Sizing pumps based on total points and line length.
* **Grease/Oil Metering:** Calculating precise discharge per cycle to prevent over-lubrication.
* **Key Parameters:** Operating pressure, pump flow rate, and lubricant NLGI grade.

### **2. Advanced Filtration & Strainers**
Strategic selection of **Local (Indian)** and **International** protection systems:
* **Duplex Strainers:** Ensuring zero-downtime during filter element changes.
* **High-Purity Filtration:** Utilizing **Pall** or **Hydac** for sub-micron requirements in MGPS and hydraulic circuits.
* **Local Value Engineering:** Integrating cost-effective **Filpro** or local equivalents for secondary pre-filtration.
* **Calculations:** $ΔP$ (Pressure Drop), Beta Ratio ($β$), and micron rating ($μ$) vs. flow velocity.

### **3. Fabrication & Combustion**
* **Structural:** Utilizing **IS 2062** for high-tensile reliability in industrial skids.
* **Thermal:** Optimization of burners and fuel-to-air ratios for minimum emission and maximum BTU output.
