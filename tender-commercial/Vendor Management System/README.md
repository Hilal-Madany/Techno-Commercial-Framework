# 🏛️ Core Industry Vendor Assessment System (F-VEN-2025-01)
**Multi-Sector Edition: Indian Railways | EPC | PSU | Government & Defence**

This repository contains the official framework for **Techno-Commercial Vendor Assessment**. It is designed to standardise the registration process for suppliers in high-stakes sectors like Railways, Power, and Infrastructure.

---

## 📄 Document Overview
The primary document in this repository, **F-VEN-2025-01**, is a 22-page comprehensive assessment sheet. It covers:
* **Part A-B:** Basic Particulars & Statutory Registrations (PAN/GST/MSME).
* **Part C-E:** Technical Capability, Machinery & Infrastructure.
* **Part F:** Financial Soundness (3-Year Audited Turnover & Net Worth).
* **Part G:** Quality Management Systems (ISO 9001/14001/45001).
* **Part L:** Official Scoring Matrix & Evaluation Guidelines.

---

## 📊 Evaluation & Star Rating System
The evaluation is based on a **100-point scale**. Vendors are categorized based on their total compliance score:

* **⭐ Approved (≥ 70%):** Vendor meets all criteria; issue approval letter.
* **🟡 Conditionally Approved (50–69%):** Provisional approval; review required within 12 months.
* **❌ Not Approved (< 50%):** Rejection; vendor may re-apply after 6 months with improvements.



---

## 🛠️ Instructions for Use
1. **Download:** Access the `F-VEN-2025-01` PDF from the root directory.
2. **Fill:** All columns must be filled. Use 'NA' where not applicable.
3. **Audit:** Ensure all "★" marked items are supported by documentary evidence as per the **Part K Checklist**.
4. **Scoring:** Internal procurement teams should use **Part L** to award marks.
